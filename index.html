Why LED?

▔

Facts in Puerto Rico

It's no joke that the cost of energy has skyrocketed in the past years, with no end in sight. In the United States, residents pay around 13¢ per kWh for their energy. In Puerto Rico, we pay twice this amount.

Energy conservation initiatives include:


1.  Turning off lights.

2.  Solar energy-related devices (e.g. solar panels).

3. Switching to more energy-efficient light bulbs!

Government initiatives to promote the reduction of energy in electricity consumption has been limited compared to the US. Repeated increases in the price of oil during this past ''lost decade'' have made it clear that when it comes to energy independence, it is time to talk less and to do more.

Features and Benefits of LEDs

There are myriad reasons to switch your lighting system to LED lights:


Saves energy: LED lights use 50% to 90% less power than traditional lighting.

Saves money.

Saves the environment: Less waste and disposal hazards.

Instant On: No warm-up time!

Lasts 5 to 10 times longer.

Generates less heat.

Eco-friendly: No harsh chemicals (e.g. mercury).

Reduced maintenance cost.

Dark sky compliant.

Controllable: Able to set controls at individual light.

Better visibility: White light has better Color Rendering Index (CRI).

Multiple color options: From 2,700 K (warm) to 6,500 K (cool) white.

No UV radiation: Produces no Ultraviolet or Infrared light!

More durable: Breakage and vibration resistant.

Produces less glare: Higher productivity and safety.

Better directional lighting.

The list goes on and on...
