About Us

▔

JLM Solutions Corporation is an energy efficiency-oriented company focused on providing top-notch environmentally friendly products to its clients. We strive to be the leader in the industry and provide the latest technologies and advances available that can benefit the end user.

Privately held for the Puerto Rico and Caribbean markets, it was established in 2012 by Eng. Enrique Hernández and Eng. Miguel P. Vélez, PE. Eng. Hernández, a graduate of Georgia Tech with a Master's Degree in Electrical Engineering, has been involved in the electronic and automotive industries and representation of electronic and electrical products. Eng. Vélez, another graduate of Georgia Tech with a Bachelor's Degree in Civil Engineering and a licenced Professional Engineer, has been involved in the construction industry in Puerto Rico and abroad.
